Lotto is an on-line Game utlilising a computerised terminal to recordselections and also validate prize claims. Participants may select 6 out of 52 numbers that can be entered into a draw. The live draw takes place twice a week, Wednesday and Saturday.

PowerBall

Powerball is an on-line Game utlilising a computerised terminal to record selections and also validate prize claims. Participants may select 5 out of 50 numbers and 1 number out of 20 numbers that can be entered into a draw. The live draw takes place twice a week, Tuesday and Friday.
